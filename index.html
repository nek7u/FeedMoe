FeedMoe

FeedMoe is a skin for Rainmeter.
Rainmeter is a free and open-source desktop customization utility for Windows.

Features

  • Simple skin for RSS Feeds. RSS2.0 and some Atom formats.
  • Keyword filtering, URL filtering.
  • Low cpu usage.
  • Plugin FeedReader.dll
